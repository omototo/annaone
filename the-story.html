The Story of Anna

Feb 2020

The story of Anna started in 2020, during the first COVID-19 lockdown. Working from home with kids and no access to cleaning services became a challenge nobody foresaw. This issue raised the need for a household robot.

Jun 2020

The development team began assembling the basic prototype. The Anna robot started as a 3D-printed hand, then came a platform, and after that, the very first model of Anna was presented to the leadership team.

Dec 2020

The idea of developing a cleaning robot was not new, but the real challenge was to have a robot that performs the tasks and is produced at a low price. Luckily, recent innovations that pushed the cost of advanced technology down allowed building a robot accessible to many customers.

May 2021

To make a robot that can do diverse tasks and quickly adapt to various environments in people's homes, the team decided on hybrid intelligence: an AI robot operated and trained by a human.

August 2021

The team finalized the first model of the robot Anna and is eager to transition into production. The team is currently looking for investors to finance this transition and bring Anna into every home.

September 2021

Basic functions test

May 2022

AnnaOne was equipped with her first set of custom-made coverings, and her tactile world expanded dramatically. With the integration of innovative resistor sensors in her hands and our proprietary solution inside her gloves, we've enriched the operator's tactile experience, marking a new milestone in interactive home robotics.

September 2022

Anna took a bold step into the warehousing sector. Equipped with a fully-developed and tested SLAM system, she now navigates with precision and autonomy, freeing up operators to focus on what really matters: efficient and accurate picking. This marks a new chapter in Anna's journey, proving her versatility and adaptability beyond the home environment.
